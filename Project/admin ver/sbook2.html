Organic Chemistry

    
    
    Auther: Jonathan clayden
            Nick Greeves
            Stuart Warren

    Category: Science books

    price: $60 

    Availability:  Not Available
    
 About  the book:

    This book, Volume 23 in the Tetrahedron Organic Chemistry series, presents organolithium chemistry from the perspective of a 
    synthetic organic chemist, drawing from the synthetic literature to present a unified overview of how organolithiums (compounds 
    in which there is a clear carbon-lithium bond) can be used to make molecules.The twin themes of reactivity and selectivity run 
    through the book, which reviews the ways by which organolithiums may be formed and the ways in which they react.
